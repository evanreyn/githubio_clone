David Bercovici

PI Yale University

Web page

The focus of my research is on mantle convection, lithosphere dynamics and the origin of plate tectonics, hotspots and mantle plumes, and volcanic flows and eruptions. I also research multi-phase flows that are being applied to both mantle melting as well as carbon sequestration and shale-gas development.

My roles in this project include implementing plate boundary dynamics, plus mantle water and carbon cycling into Open Earth Systems.
