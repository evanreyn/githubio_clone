Michael Manga

PI University of California, Berkeley

Web page

My focus in the Open Earth Systems Project is on understanding how tectonic and dynamic processes affect melt production and storage in the mantle and at the base of the crust, melt transport through the crust, and eruption at the surface. As part of this project we are developing new techniques to determine the locations and timing of large basaltic eruptions.
